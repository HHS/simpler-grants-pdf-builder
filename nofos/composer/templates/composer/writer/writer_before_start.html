Before you get started

We’ve made it easier to set up your NOFO so you can focus on clear, accurate information. Before you begin, here’s what you’ll need and what to expect.

  1. Gather some basic information

    Before you start, have a few key details ready about your program.
    Composer uses this information to tailor the questions you’ll see and structure your NOFO.

    You’ll need:

    • Program title
    • Federal assistance listing
    • Statutory authority
    • Activity code (if applicable)

    Having this information ready will make setup faster.

  2. Fill out the guided questions

    Composer will walk you through short questions for each step of the NOFO.
    You can save your progress anytime and return later.

    Steps include:

    1. Review the opportunity
    2. Get ready to apply
    3. Build your application
    4. Understand review, selection, and award
    5. Submit your application
    6. Learn what happens after award
    7. Contacts and support

  3. Review and make edits

    As you go, you can review and edit your responses at any time.
    Your answers shape how the NOFO appears when you and your team start writing the main sections.

  4. Save and preview your NOFO

    When you feel ready, you can download and continue your NOFO draft in Word, following the instructions in each section.
