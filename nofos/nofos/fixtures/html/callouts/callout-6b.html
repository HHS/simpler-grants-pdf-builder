Purpose

The purpose of the Rape Prevention and Education (RPE) program is to prevent sexual violence (SV) perpetration and victimization by increasing protective factors and decreasing risk factors. This can be achieved through a public health approach that implements and evaluates comprehensive strategies based on the best available evidence.
